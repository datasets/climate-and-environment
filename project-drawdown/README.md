# Project Drawdown Climate Solutions

Ranked table of ~150 climate solutions analysed by [Project Drawdown](https://drawdown.org), a leading research organisation that identifies and models the most effective ways to reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

## Data

The dataset contains 156 solutions across sectors including Electricity, Transportation, Buildings & Industry, and Food, Agriculture, Land & Ocean (FALO). Each solution is classified and, where data is available, includes:

- **GHG Impact** — estimated reduction in Gt CO₂-equivalent per year over a 100-year horizon
- **Cost** — net cost in USD per tonne of CO₂-equivalent (negative = net savings)
- **Mode** — whether the solution cuts emissions, removes carbon, or both
- **Speed of Action** — Emergency Brake, Gradual, or Delayed
